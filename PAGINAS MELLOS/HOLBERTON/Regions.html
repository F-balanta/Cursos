Explore and Discover Runeterra

The Freljord

The Freljord is a harsh and unforgiving region where demigods walk the earth and people are born warriors. While there are several individual tribes, the three largest are the Greed, the Winter Claw, and the Ice Guard, each modeled after their will to survive. It is also the only place in Runeterra where Pure Ice can be found.

Demacia

Demacia is a proud and lawful kingdom with a prestigious military history. It was founded to be a refuge from magic after the Runic Wars, but there are those who claim that its golden age has come to an end, unless it is able to adapt to an ever-changing world. Self-sufficient and agrarian, Demacian society is by nature insular and defensive; they value justice, honor and duty above all things.

Noxus

Noxus is a brutal and expansionist empire, but those who look beyond its warlike facade will find an atypically inclusive society. Anyone can rise to a position of power and respect if they present the necessary aptitude, regardless of their social status, background or wealth. Noxians value strength above all things; however, that force can manifest itself in several different ways.

Piltover y Zaun

Dual city-states that control the main trade routes between Valoran and Shurima. Being the home of visionary inventors with rich patrons, the division between social classes becomes more dangerous every day.
