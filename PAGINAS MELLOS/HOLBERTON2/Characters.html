Characters

Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki (う ず ま き ナ ル ト, Uzumaki Naruto) is the protagonist of the manga and anime series Naruto and Naruto Shippūden. He is also a participant in the manga and anime Boruto: Naruto Next Generations, a sequel to the original work by Masashi Kishimoto. He is a resident of Konohagakure, son of the Fourth Hokage, Minato Namikaze and his wife Kushina Uzumaki, as well as being the current reincarnation of Asura Ōtsutsuki. From the day of his birth until he was extracted from him by Madara Uchiha he was the Jinchūriki of the Yang half of Kurama and the most recent of the Yin half of Kurama. He would eventually receive the extracted half of Kurama, merging with the other half of him inside it. He also possesses part of the Chakra of the other Tailed Beasts, becoming a Jinchūriki for each of the Bijū and for these reasons he has been named as the Human Pillar of the Power of the Ten Tails. [14] He was a member of Team Kakashi alongside Sakura Haruno and Sasuke Uchiha. He is currently the Seventh Hokage (七 代 目 火影, Nanadaime Hokage; which means "Seventh Shadow of Fire") of Konoha, fulfilling his dream, as well as the husband of Hinata Hyūga and father of Boruto Uzumaki and Himawari Uzumaki.

Sakura Haruno

Sakura Haruno(春 野 サ ク ラ, Haruno Sakura) whose current name is Sakura Uchiha (う ち は サ ク ラ, Uchiha Sakura) is one of the main characters in the series. She is a Jōnin-level kunoichi, a member of Team Kakashi and a great friend of Naruto Uzumaki. After training her with Tsunade, she becomes a Medical Ninja. She eventually became the wife of Sasuke Uchiha and the mother of Sarada Uchiha.

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha (う ち は サ ス ケ, Uchiha Sasuke) is the deuteragonist of the series and one of the survivors of the Uchiha Clan. He is the son of Fugaku Uchiha and Mikoto Uchiha, Itachi Uchiha's brother, as well as the current reincarnation of Indra Ōtsutsuki. Despite building relationships with members of Team Kakashi, Sasuke decided to defect from Konohagakure in search of the power necessary to avenge his clan and assassinate Itachi. After discovering the truth about his brother, he founded Taka, was a temporary ally of Akatsuki, and became an international criminal. After talking with Hashirama Senju, he decided to follow his brother's dream, collaborate with the Allied Shinobi Forces and set himself a goal to carry out a revolution in the Shinobi World. After the Fourth Shinobi World War, he would end up giving in to the mentality of Naruto Uzumaki, returning to the village and dedicating his life to protecting it and its inhabitants, becoming referred to as the Support Kage (支 う 影, Sasaukage; literally: Shadow of support). Likewise, he became Sakura Haruno's husband and Sarada Uchiha's father.
