Naruto

Naruto (ナ ル ト?), Romanized as NARUTO, is a manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. The play tells the story of a teenage ninja named Naruto Uzumaki, who aspires to become the Hokage, leader of his village, with the purpose of being recognized as someone important within the village and among his companions.

Data of Naruto

Naruto is one of the best-known manga series today and, among other things, it has two direct sequels: Naruto Shippūden and Boruto: Naruto Next Generations. The manga, created by Masashi Kishimoto, has two anime adaptations, and the third (Boruto: Naruto Next Generations) will premiere on April 5, 2017. The manga began publication in 1999 in Shūkan Shōnen Jump, and ended in November. from 2014; The series has been compiled into a total of 72 tankōbon.

Synopsis

Currently, the world is divided into several countries mostly governed by the military power of the hidden villages, in charge of training and educating the most valued military forces in the world: shinobi and kunoichi. These, armed with special techniques, usually offer their services in exchange for money while competing with each other.

Naruto Uzumaki is a young shinobi from Konohagakure no Sato who dreams of becoming a Hokage. In the process, he graduates from the academy and becomes part of Team 7 alongside Kakashi Hatake, Sakura Haruno, and Sasuke Uchiha. On the other hand, Uzumaki is the recent Kyūbi, a legendary and bloody beast that devastated everything in its path. Together with his new companions, he approaches a story full of action, adventure, betrayal and pain. The world is in constant war, and only the strongest will be able to survive ...
